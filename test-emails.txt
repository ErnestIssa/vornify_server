EMAIL TESTING COMMANDS
======================

Replace YOUR_EMAIL@example.com with your actual email address

1. TEST ALL EMAIL TEMPLATES AT ONCE
   curl "http://localhost:10000/api/email-test/test-all?email=YOUR_EMAIL@example.com"

2. VERIFY SENDGRID CONNECTION
   curl http://localhost:10000/api/email/verify

3. TEST INDIVIDUAL EMAIL TEMPLATES
   curl "http://localhost:10000/api/email-test/test/confirmation?email=YOUR_EMAIL@example.com"
   curl "http://localhost:10000/api/email-test/test/processing?email=YOUR_EMAIL@example.com"
   curl "http://localhost:10000/api/email-test/test/shipping?email=YOUR_EMAIL@example.com"
   curl "http://localhost:10000/api/email-test/test/delivery?email=YOUR_EMAIL@example.com"
   curl "http://localhost:10000/api/email-test/test/review?email=YOUR_EMAIL@example.com"
   curl "http://localhost:10000/api/email-test/test/newsletter-welcome?email=YOUR_EMAIL@example.com"
   curl "http://localhost:10000/api/email-test/test/discount-reminder?email=YOUR_EMAIL@example.com"

4. CHECK ENVIRONMENT VARIABLES
   curl http://localhost:10000/api/email-test/debug-env
